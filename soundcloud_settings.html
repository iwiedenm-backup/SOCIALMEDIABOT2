Quick Start Guide

Recommended actions

Do not use all of the actions at the same time as a beginner. It is safer to use only the Like action on Slow speed for your first few days on Instagress.

Recommended speed

Start using Instagress on the Slow speed to ensure your account is safe. You can increase the speed to Normal or Fast after a few days of using Instagress.

Scheduling your activity

We recommend that you schedule only 6 hours per day with the Scheduling feature as a beginner. Be sure to randomize the time gaps each day so your activity looks natural.

Adjust your settings

Instagress offers a wide variety of activity settings for the most effective interaction with your target audience.

Instagram content

It's important that your Instagram account doesn't look fake or spammy. It is better to have an actively maintained account with at least 15 photos/videos posted.

Important information

Feel free to post pictures when Instagress is started but make sure you don't Like, Comment, Follow, or Unfollow manually. Manual activity can lead to password resets.

Now you are ready!

It's time to customize your settings and start your activity.

Activity Error Help

Your Activity stopped with error? These can be the possible reasons:

  • No connection to Instagram

    How to solve: Try to restart your Activity after a while.

    This error may occur due to temporary networking issues on our servers or when Instagram is temporarily unavailable.

  • Unable to perform selected action

    How to solve: Try to restart your Activity using Slow or Normal speed after 12-24 hours.

    It looks like Instagram has temporarily blocked one of your actions (like, comment, follow or unfollow) due to high speed of your activity.

  • Access to Instagram has been reset

    How to solve: Try to reconnect your account after a while and then restart your Activity.

    This error may occur when access token or password of your username was reset by Instagram due to high speed of your activity or if you using Instagram on your mobile device or with third-party websites.

If you will continue to receive any activity errors, please contact us.

Sign in Help

Having trouble logging in with your Instagram credentials? These can be the possible reasons:

  • Wrong username or password

    Check that you have not misspelled your username or password. Please, note that you should enter your username without the @ symbol before it.

  • Two-factor authentication

    You have two-factor authentication enabled on your Instagram account. A security code should be sent by Instagram to your mobile number. You need to enter it to complete second authentication step.

  • Integrity checkpoint

    This error means that Instagram is asking you to verify your account. You must be logged in on the Instagram website with your Instagram account to do it.

  • Instagram login error

    It looks like Instagram requires to additionally verify your account. There could be several verification options. Please, login on the Instagram website using your credentials to complete the additional verification.

  • Instagram password reset

    In rare cases Instagram can reset your password when you're trying to login on third-party websites. Go to your email (associated with your Instagram account) and check your inbox/spam folders for a message from Instagram with a password reset link.

If you continue to receive any login errors, please contact us.
